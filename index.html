About Me

Hi, I'm Nick – a UX designer from Birmingham, England. I'm currently employed at Pinewood Technologies plc, where I champion clean, intuitive UI across our products.

I'm an expert in HTML5, CSS3, SASS, JavaScript (mainly jQuery library, but I'm slowly getting there with Knockout.js), responsive design/builds, performance optimisation and more. I always strive to write clean, semantic code to bring designs to life.

I am a creative communicator with a keen eye for detail, a passion for developing ideas and experience of implementing change within organisations.
